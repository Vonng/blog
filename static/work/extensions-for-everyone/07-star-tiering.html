Group extensions by order of magnitude, and we get a simple tier model. Tier zero — the magnificent four. PostGIS, TimescaleDB, pgvector, Citus. More than ten thousand stars each. And a long tail of around 748 extensions below ten stars. This is not a quality ranking. Some popular projects are no longer active, like pgml or zombodb. Some low-star extensions are quite useful. But the tiers tell us something. The visible ecosystem is much smaller than the discovered one. Adding tiers zero through three gives about 570 extensions with more than ten stars — close to what is actually deliverable.
